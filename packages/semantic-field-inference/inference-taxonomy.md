# Semantic Field Inference Taxonomy

This taxonomy defines the provisional field categories that the governed scaffold may emit.

## Core Categories

- `date-or-period-field`
- `role-or-subject-field`
- `quantity-or-metric-field`
- `observation-field`
- `possible-identifier-field`
- `administrative-descriptor-field`
- `unclassified-structural-field`

## Evidence Styles

- `simple-label-pattern`
- `compound-label-pattern`
- `multi-token-label-pattern`

## Confidence Levels

- `high`
- `medium`
- `low`

## Governance Notes

- Categories are provisional and structural
- Categories must not be treated as extracted facts
- Identifier-like detection must remain generic and must not capture real identifiers
- Unclear labels should remain unclassified or routed to review
